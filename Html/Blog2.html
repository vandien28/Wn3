HAC FAMES TEMPOR
Posted on 29/12/2018 by GiaoDienWeb

Alcohol is not a poison to be completely avoided, but it is necessary to "avoid" when drinking with a woman sitting next to it.

PV : How much alcohol do you think you should drink?

Experts Vũ Thế Thành: Not more or less, I am also a person… alcohol. Answering the question, how much alcohol is appropriate, I am afraid that because of "related interests", I cannot answer objectively.

Well, let science recommend it, it will be more fair. But scientists, depending on whether people are easy or difficult, give very different numbers.

I quote here the recommendation of scientists at the Mayo Clinic, one of the famous non-profit research and treatment facilities in the US. They recommend drinking no more than 148ml of wine, equivalent to less than half a liter of beer.

That's the largest number of drinking recommendations I've rummaged through. I don't see them mocking men or women at all.

PV : A question that goes a bit beyond science, what do you think about drinking?

Experts Vũ Thế Thành: First, I want to share a little bit with the "comrades" of alcohol. Alcohol is not a poison to be completely avoided, but it is necessary to "avoid" when drinking with a woman sitting next to it. The spirit of speech harms the mortal body. Innate, women remember tough, talk tough, and can end their lives if they… are tough. The headache effect is more than the acetaldehyde residue in alcohol metabolism.

The consolation is that the first famous monk, Hai Thuong Lan Ong, said: "Selling da tam smeared wine... The doctor is not unique, that is, if you have three glasses of wine at night, you can take a break from the doctor's day." However, the size of the wine glass, I did not see him mention. The old wine glass must have been as small as a tea cup. If so, maybe even "stricter" than the scientists at the Mayo Clinic.

Wine is, after all, just a means to deepen conversation. A glass or two of wine is enough. The ancients said: Food and drink harmonize the gentleman. The question is whether or not you can control yourself after drinking alcohol.
